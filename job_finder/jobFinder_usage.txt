usage: jobFinder.py [-h] --job_sites JOB_SITES [--location LOCATION]
                    --keywords KEYWORDS [--apply APPLY] [--filename FILENAME]

Finding jobs on nijobs.com or nijobfinder.com can be a pain, so this script
will do it for you: just give what sites you want to search, keywords to
include, and it will return links to the most prevelant job postings. More
site to follow.

optional arguments:
  -h, --help            show this help message and exit
  --job_sites JOB_SITES
                        job sites to scrape from
  --location LOCATION   Location of job search
  --keywords KEYWORDS   keywords to search in job titles; separate with ','
  --apply APPLY
  --filename FILENAME   filepath of file to transfer
